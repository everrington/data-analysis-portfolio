Mock Test Physics
TPAT3 Dashboard

Google Looker Studio Data Visualization Education Analytics

Physics Blueprint runs competitive mock examinations for Physics TPAT3 students. After each test, hundreds of students receive a raw score — but raw scores alone don't tell students where they lost marks, how they compare to peers, or which physics topics need the most attention.

This project built a 3-page Google Looker Studio report that transforms raw test data into a personalized, actionable performance breakdown for every student. Each student can see their score item-by-item, understand how their performance breaks down across the 5 physics topic groups, and view analytics including their percentile rank within the cohort and a radar chart showing strength and weakness by subject area.

Role

Dashboard designer & data analyst — end-to-end from data preparation to Looker Studio layout

Stack

Google Looker Studio · Google Sheets · Google Forms

Audience

Physics TPAT3 students sitting mock exams at Physics Blueprint

Data

30-item exam · 244 students · 5 physics topic groups · difficulty-tagged items

Dashboard features
Page 1 — Score by item: Full 30-item breakdown showing each question's chapter, topic, and score. Difficulty tags (ง่าย / กลางๆ / ยาก) let students identify whether they're losing marks on hard or easy questions.
Page 2 — Score by physics group: Results grouped into 5 topic areas — กลศาสตร์, คลื่นกลและแสง, ไฟฟ้าแม่เหล็ก, อุณหพลศาสตร์, and ฟิสิกส์แผนใหม่ — with chapter-level breakdowns and percentage scores within each group.
Page 3 — Analytics: Score distribution bar chart, cohort percentile bar showing where the student ranks among all 244 participants, KPI cards, and a radar chart overlaying the student's topic profile against the cohort maximum.
